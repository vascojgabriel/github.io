About Me

I joined the Department of Economics at the University of Victoria, Canada, in 2021 as Professor and Department Chair. I am also a Visiting Researcher at the Department of Financial Stability, Bank of Portugal and a Researcher at the Centre for Research in Economics and Management - NIPE, University of Minho.

I graduated in Economics from ISEG, Lisbon School of Economics & Management, University of Lisbon in 1995, where I was awarded the ICEP prize.

I received a masters degree in Applied Maths (Econometrics) in 1998 from the same institution. In 2002, I completed a PhD in Economics at Birkbeck College, University of London.

I taught at the University of Minho, Portugal, before joining the University of Surrey in 2004, where I have been Head of School, Director of Learning and Teaching, Director of Postgraduate Studies at Surrey, and co-founded CIMS, the Centre for International Macroeconomic Studies.
